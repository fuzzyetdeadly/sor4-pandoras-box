# Pandora's box (an SOR4 and Absolum editor)

<div align="center">
  <img src="./assets/images/general/PandorasBoxAbout.png" alt="Pandora's box" />
</div>

**Pandora's box** is a powerful modding tool that allows you to directly modify data configurations of **Streets of Rage 4** (SOR4) and **Absolum** via their **bigfile**.

The *bigfile* contains configurations that control the majority of the behaviors in the game.

It was used to develop [**SOR4: Reignited**](https://gamebanana.com/mods/473047) (by the **SoulFire team**) and is being used to develop **SOR4: Turbo** (by Bragdras).

The [data structures](./technical/data-structures.md) for the supported games are very complex. Thus, _Pandora's box_ is designed to have minimal "training wheels" to maximize modding flexibility to the user. There **WILL** be a learning curve, so be sure to pace yourself with learning about the nuances of modding this game so you don't burn out.

*Pandora's box* was created from scratch after reverse engineering the [SOR4 Swapper](https://gamebanana.com/tools/7320) and [SOR4Explorer](https://gamebanana.com/tools/7297). A special thanks to the authors of these amazing tools, for I don't think this would have been possible without them!

Get ready to find out; _"What's in the box?"_

The latest stable release can be found [**here**](https://github.com/fuzzyetdeadly/sor4-pandoras-box/releases/latest)

## About the team

### FuzzYetDeadly (aka Glytch)

I started playing SOR4 during the pandemic, and I got pretty hooked. I mostly did score-running in v7 of the game back then, and have achieved most of my goals already. Since then, my passion has shifted towards making mods for the game, and enabling others to do the same.

Thus, the idea for *Pandora's box* was born. Work began in mid 2023, and will be ongoing at a pace that is reasonable for the creators. There are many more features I would still like to add to the tool, but I foresee it's going to take a long time.

## Maintainer's notes

To manage expectations a bit, I don't have a lot of free time on my hands to *update the contents of this document* and *release updates to the tool*. I will likely be doing these on a best effort basis. Don't be surprised if it might be months to years between updates.

The *bigfiles* for both games are very complex, and it is difficult to understand everything about them. Much of what is known is found through discovery by just changing things and checking what happened in game.

If there is something you wish to mod but aren't sure how; I recommend seeking out other modders for the game of your interest. 

### SOR4

_SoulFire Team's_ [Discord server](https://discord.gg/UzrMtCD7y9) is intended to be a hub specialized towards modding discussions for *SOR4*. It is also where you can access nightly builds (WIP developments) of *Pandora's box*, and have discussions about how to use it. 

Do say "Hi" after you join so I know you're not a bot, then I will assign you a role so you can see the channels in the server.

Find SOR4 mods (not necessarily made with Pandora's box) at:

* [GameBanana](https://gamebanana.com/games/8173)
* [NexusMods](https://www.nexusmods.com/games/streetsofrage4)

### Absolum 

For Absolum modding discussion, there is a modding thread at the [DOTEMU discord server](https://discord.com/invite/RPxFsWMTdA). You will first need to join, then follow access the thread via [this](https://discord.com/channels/1358775517385461952/1450848894505455636/1450848898536177815) link. You may wish to right click the thread, and set `Notification settings = always`, as this will improve the visibility of the thread that tends to disappear after some time.

Find Absolum mods at:

* [NexusMods](https://www.nexusmods.com/games/absolum)
